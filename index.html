Why should you adopt?

Adopting a cat not only gives a loving home to a furry friend in need but also brings joy and companionship into your life. Its a win-win situation! By adopting, you are helping to reduce the number of homeless animals and supporting animal welfare organizations. Plus, you'll have the satisfaction of knowing you've made a positive impact on an animal's life while gaining a loyal and loving companion.

Adoption Process Steps
  1. Complete the adoption quiz to find your purrfect match.
  2. Come in for an interview with our staff.
  3. Allow our staff to do a home visit and background check.
  4. Meet and greet your potential new companion.
  5. Complete the adoption paperwork and pay fees.
  6. Take home your new fluffy companion!
Can't adopt? Check out our volunteer program instead!

If you're unable to adopt, consider volunteering with us! Our volunteer program offers opportunities to help care for cats in our shelter, assist with adoption events, and support our mission of finding loving homes for all animals. Volunteers are essential to our success and make a real difference in the lives of cats in need.

Volunteer Opportunities:
  • 1. Cat Care: Help feed, groom, and play with our cats.
  • 2. Adoption Events: Assist with setting up and running adoption events.
  • 3. Fundraising: Support our fundraising efforts to provide care for our cats.
  • 4. Outreach: Help spread the word about our shelter and adoption opportunities.
